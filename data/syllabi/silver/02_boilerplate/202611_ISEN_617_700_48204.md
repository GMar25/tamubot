## Catalog Description


Quantitative Models for Supply Chain Coordination. (3-0). Credit 3. Concepts, complexities, and models pertaining to supply chain management and relation of those to recent practical initiatives; includes channel coordination models, supply chain contracting, and vendor-managed, inventory models.




## Course Learning Outcomes


Upon completion of this course, the learner will be able to:

- model operations processes involving inventory and transportation costs in multistage systems;

- understand operations tradeoffs and conflicting incentives at different stages in a supply chain;

- apply a set of tools and policies needed to coordinate operations at different supply chain stages.
